Custom Components

This site is a showcase of custom components built on Angular v21, fully guided by Atomic Design principles. From Atoms, Molecules, and Organisms to reusable page compositions, it demonstrates a consistent, scalable, and maintainable component system. You can explore each component’s interaction behavior, styling conventions, and real-world usage scenarios to help teams deliver high-quality interfaces faster while maintaining readability, testability, performance, and long-term maintainability.
